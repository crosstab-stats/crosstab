Messy-data fidelity spike — DuckDB-WASM ↔ WebR

Real survey/admin data is messy: missing cells, SPSS user-missing codes (e.g. -99), categorical factors with value labels, unicode names, and columns that look numeric but contain junk. This spike builds a tiny dataset with all of those — where every expected value is known — and asserts that both bridges (JS-arrays and Parquet) carry it into R faithfully. The cleaning rules are driven by app-side variable metadata and pushed down into DuckDB SQL. Needs cross-origin isolation.
